# Customer Behavior Analysis in Amazon E-Commerce

This repository contains an interactive **Power BI dashboard** designed to analyze customer behavior in Amazon E-Commerce. The project focuses on understanding shopping patterns, identifying factors that influence customer decisions, and providing actionable insights to improve customer satisfaction and business performance.

---

## Key Features

- **Customer Demographics Analysis:** Explored customer distribution based on age and gender.
- **Shopping Behavior Analysis:** Analyzed purchase frequency, browsing behavior, and shopping satisfaction.
- **Cart Abandonment Analysis:** Identified the main reasons for cart abandonment, including high prices and shipping costs.
- **Customer Reviews Analysis:** Examined customer feedback and review importance in purchasing decisions.
- **Product Category Analysis:** Identified the most popular product categories among customers.
- **Personalized Recommendations:** Analyzed customer responses to personalized product recommendations.

---

## Impact

Provided data-driven insights to improve customer experience, reduce cart abandonment, optimize marketing strategies, and support business decisions that increase customer engagement and sales performance.

---

## Technologies

- **Power BI** for data analysis and interactive dashboards.

---

## Business Recommendations

- Offer competitive pricing and promotional discounts to reduce cart abandonment.
- Provide free or discounted shipping for eligible orders.
- Improve customer service to increase satisfaction and trust.
- Leverage AI-driven personalized recommendations to enhance customer engagement.
- Focus marketing campaigns on high-demand product categories and frequent shoppers.
